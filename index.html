SOCRadar TCHELEBI Wide Scan project is a subproject inside of our platform. We are constantly looking for open ports and banners of our customers' external ip addresses to generate alarms for any potential misconfiguration.

Our carefully designed system send TCP SYN packets in a very low rate (lower than 0.1 Mbps) and scans are distributed and randomized over the time to prevent any load on the destination ip addresses.
